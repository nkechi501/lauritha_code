Benz

Mercedes-Benz is a German luxury car company that produces vehicles known for their opulence and state-of-the-art technology. Many are available as sedans and cabriolets (convertibles), and some Mercedes luxury SUVs, such as the GLC and GLE, are available as coupes. Mercedes also has high-performance AMG variants for many of its vehicles, including the GLA, GLS, G-Class, E-Class, and C-Class. Vehicles from BMW, Porsche, Lexus, and Jaguar are all commonly compared with Mercedes models.
